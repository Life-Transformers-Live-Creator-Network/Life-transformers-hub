Pro Tip for Creators

Regular breaks and self-care aren't luxuries—they're essential for sustainable success. Take 5-10 minutes between streams to reset, hydrate, and breathe. Your audience benefits from a refreshed, energized you.

Self-Care Reminders

Stay Hydrated

Keep water nearby during streams. Dehydration affects mood and energy.

Take Breaks

Step away from screens every hour. Your eyes and mind need rest.

Sleep Matters

Aim for 7-9 hours. Late-night streams are tough—prioritize recovery time.

Connect with Others

Don't isolate. Talk to friends, family, or our LIFE community regularly.

You're Not Alone

The LIFE community is here to support you. Whether you're celebrating wins or facing challenges, reach out. Your wellbeing matters to us.
