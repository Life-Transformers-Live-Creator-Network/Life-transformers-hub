💡 Tips for Using Music Effectively

Best Practices:

  • Match music energy to your content's pace and mood
  • Keep background music at 20-30% volume so your voice is clear
  • Use trending TikTok sounds when possible for better reach
  • Add sound effects to emphasize key moments
  • Test audio levels before posting - listen with headphones
  • Save your favorite tracks for quick access
